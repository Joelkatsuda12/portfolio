EMU Transfer Student Posters


In this project, I was told to create a series of informational flyers promoting several welcoming events for EMU transfer students. Consistency was very important to me for this project, since the transfer students were likely to view each flier. As a result, small details such as background imagery and font sizes were important to me in order to offer a welcoming tone to new students.
